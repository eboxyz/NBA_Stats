NBA Name Game

How well do you know the faces of the NBA?

Rules:

You have 90 seconds to put a name to a face for each player. If you enter just the last name correctly, you get one point. If you enter the full name correctly, you get two points. You will lose one point for every skip you make. You can skip by pressing the ESC key. Select a team. If you guess correctly for a player on your team, you'll earn an extra point. If you guess incorrectly for a player on your team, you'll lose an extra point.
